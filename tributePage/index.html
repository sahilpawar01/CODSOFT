Dr. Srinivasa Ramanujan

A Visionary Mathematician Who Transcended Boundaries

A Glimpse into His Extraordinary Life:

  • 1887: Born in Erode, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • 1904: Made groundbreaking discoveries in mathematics while still a student.
  • 1913: Gained recognition from G.H. Hardy, marking the start of his international fame.
  • 1914: Traveled to Cambridge, contributing significant mathematical theorems.
  • 1918: Elected Fellow of the Royal Society for his exceptional work in number theory.
  • 1920: Passed away at the young age of 32, leaving an unparalleled legacy in mathematics.
